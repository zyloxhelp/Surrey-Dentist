About Dr. Amira

Dr. Amira Al-Hashmi is the founder of Surrey Dental Studio and one of Dubai's most respected implantologists. After graduating with honours from King's College London and completing advanced training at the prestigious Loma Linda University in California, she returned to the UAE with a singular mission: to bring world-class dentistry to the heart of Dubai.

Over the past two decades, Dr. Amira has placed more than 5,000 implants and transformed thousands of smiles. She is a certified Straumann and Nobel Biocare specialist and regularly trains other dentists across the Middle East in advanced implantology techniques.

Her gentle approach, surgical precision, and commitment to patient comfort have earned her a loyal following — including many of the UAE's most prominent residents and visiting dignitaries.
