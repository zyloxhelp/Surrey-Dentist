Our treatment process

We follow a meticulous 4-step process to ensure the best possible outcome for every patient.

01

Consultation & 3D Scan

Comprehensive examination, CBCT scan, and personalised treatment plan with cost breakdown.

02

Implant Placement

Precise surgical placement under local anaesthesia or sedation. Same-day temporary tooth available.

03

Healing Period

3–6 months for osseointegration (implant fuses with bone). Regular check-ups during this phase.

04

Final Crown

Custom-crafted porcelain crown attached. Indistinguishable from your natural teeth.

What to expect after surgery

Most patients return to work the next day. Mild swelling and discomfort are normal for 2–3 days, manageable with prescribed medication. Soft food diet recommended for 7–10 days.

Our team provides 24/7 post-op support via WhatsApp, and follow-up appointments are scheduled at 1 week, 1 month, and 3 months to monitor healing.

Frequently asked questions

How long do dental implants last?
+
With proper care, dental implants can last a lifetime. The implant itself comes with a lifetime warranty, while the crown typically lasts 15–25 years before needing replacement.
Is the procedure painful?
+
No. The procedure is performed under local anaesthesia, so you won't feel any pain during placement. Sedation options are available for anxious patients. Post-op discomfort is mild and manageable.
Am I a candidate for dental implants?
+
Most adults with good general health and adequate jawbone are candidates. Even patients with bone loss can benefit from bone grafting or zygomatic implants. A free consultation will determine your suitability.
Does insurance cover implants?
+
Many UAE insurance plans now cover part of implant treatment. We work with all major providers including Daman, AXA, Cigna, and Bupa. Our team will check your benefits and submit claims on your behalf.
How long is the entire treatment?
+
From start to finish, dental implant treatment typically takes 4–6 months. Same-day immediate-load implants are available in select cases, allowing you to leave with a temporary tooth on the same day.
