Dental insights & tips for your family

Our DHA-licensed specialists share their top advice for maintaining healthy smiles across all ages — from toddlers to grandparents.

Brush smarter, not harder

Most people brush too hard — this wears enamel and irritates gums. Use a soft-bristled brush, angle it at 45 degrees to the gumline, and use gentle circular motions for 2 minutes, twice daily.

Flossing is non-negotiable

40% of tooth surfaces can't be reached by a toothbrush alone. Daily flossing removes plaque and food from between teeth and under the gumline — the most common places for cavities and gum disease to start.

Children's dental timeline

First tooth → first dental visit. Don't wait until your child has a full set of teeth. Early visits help children build positive associations with dentistry and allow our team to spot any developmental issues early.

The Dubai diet challenge

Dubai's cafe culture means a lot of sugary drinks and acidic juices. Try to drink these through a straw and rinse with water afterward. Wait 30 minutes before brushing after acidic foods or drinks.
