Always hiring 📍 All Dubai locations ⏱ Full-time / Part-time 🎓 All experience levels

Don't see the perfect role? Apply anyway.

At Surrey Dental Studio, we believe in building a team of exceptional people — not just filling vacancies. If you're passionate about dentistry, patient care, or making our clinic run smoother, we want to hear from you, even if there's no specific job posted right now.

We hire across all roles — clinical, administrative, marketing, technology, and patient experience. Our most successful team members didn't always start in their final role. We invest in growth, and we love discovering hidden talent.

Who we're always looking for

  • Dental Specialists — implantologists, orthodontists, periodontists, prosthodontists
  • General Dentists — DHA-licensed with 3+ years of UAE experience
  • Dental Hygienists & Therapists — passionate about preventive care
  • Dental Nurses & Assistants — strong four-handed dentistry skills
  • Front Desk & Patient Experience — multilingual, empathetic, organised
  • Practice Managers — leadership and operational excellence
  • Marketing & Content — digital natives who love healthcare storytelling
  • Lab Technicians — CAD/CAM and ceramics experience preferred

What makes Surrey Dental different

Working at Surrey Dental isn't just a job — it's a commitment to excellence in a workplace that genuinely cares about your growth and wellbeing.

  • Competitive salary — among the highest in Dubai's dental sector
  • Continuing education budget — AED 10,000 annual training stipend
  • International conference attendance — paid trips to global dental events
  • Comprehensive medical insurance — for you and your dependents
  • Performance bonuses — quarterly performance-linked rewards
  • Modern equipment — latest CBCT, iTero, microscopes, and digital workflow
  • Collaborative culture — flat hierarchy, direct access to leadership
  • Visa & relocation support — for international hires

Our hiring process

We believe in respecting your time. Our process is designed to be efficient and transparent:

  • Application review — within 5 business days
  • Initial conversation — 30-minute call with our HR team
  • Technical/clinical interview — with the relevant department head
  • Practical assessment — for clinical roles, a half-day clinical observation
  • Final discussion — meet the founding team and tour the clinic
  • Offer — within 7 days of final round

Equal opportunity employer

Surrey Dental Studio is proud to be an equal opportunity employer. We celebrate diversity and are committed to creating an inclusive workplace where every team member can thrive — regardless of background, nationality, religion, gender identity, or experience level.
